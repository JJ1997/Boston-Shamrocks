Boston Shamrocks

My name is Jessica Jacques. I am now a current Sophomore at the University of Maine and play on the Women's Ice Hockey team. Before attending Maine I played on a Junior Womens hockey team called the Boston Shamrocks. I played for the Shamrocks for 5 years including a gap year. I served as Captain twice. The Shamrocks play in a league called the JWHL (Junior Women's Hockey League) and play against some of the best hockey players in the country. Every year many players commit mostly to Division 1 but also III.
